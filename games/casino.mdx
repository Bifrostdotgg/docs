Casino games are wagering games played using your server's virtual economy. You bet coins on each round; winning pays out based on the game's odds. Losing reduces your balance. No real money is involved.

<Warning>
Casino games require the economy system to be configured. Members need a coin balance to place bets. See the [Economy](/games/economy) page for setup.
</Warning>

## Available games

### Blackjack

```
/blackjack <bet>
```

Standard Blackjack against the dealer. Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Supports Hit, Stand, Double, and Split where applicable. Blackjack (Ace + 10-value card on the first deal) pays 3:2.

### Coinflip

```
/coinflip <bet> <heads|tails>
```

Call heads or tails. 1:1 payout on a correct guess.

### Dice

```
/dice <bet> <target>
```

Roll two dice and predict whether the sum will be higher or lower than the target, or hit the target exactly. Payout scales with the probability of the outcome.

### Higher-Lower

```
/higher-lower <bet>
```

A card is revealed. Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower. A correct streak multiplies your winnings. Cash out at any point to claim your accumulated payout.

### Horse Racing

```
/horse-racing <bet> <horse>
```

Bet on one of several horses in a simulated race. Each horse has different odds. The race plays out over several rounds with position updates posted in the message.

### Mines

```
/mines <bet> <mine_count>
```

A grid of tiles hides a set number of mines. Reveal tiles to increase your multiplier, then cash out before hitting a mine. More mines means higher risk and higher potential reward.

### Roulette

```
/roulette <bet> <type>
```

Bet on a number, a colour (red/black), or a range (odd/even, 1–18, 19–36). A virtual wheel spins and payouts follow standard roulette odds: single numbers pay 35:1, colours pay 1:1.

## Bet limits

The minimum and maximum bet per game are configured in **Games > Configuration > Economy**. Members cannot bet more than their current balance.
